Rules:

  1. You need a field to create a sheep.
  2. For sheep to mate, you need to place a female and a male sheep in the same row.
  3. Mating has a 2/3 chance of creating a lamb.
  4. Click a sheep to brand it.
  5. Branded sheep will not mate with any other sheep.
  6. Lambs cannot be branded.
  7. A lamb will randomly become a female or male sheep when it grows up.
  8. You can't have two fields with the same name, but this doesn't apply to sheep.
  9. Mating takes 5 seconds.
  10. The cooldown between mating attempts is 8 seconds.
  11. A lamb needs 12 seconds to grow up.
  12. Don't leave your browser for too long, because the sheep will take over your CPU and RAM.
